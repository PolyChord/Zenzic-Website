Optimising the number of antennas

During our work with Warwick Manufacturing Group, we optimised the placement of the antennas on the Warwick campus to yield the best possible network coverage of the main road network to facilitate the deployment of connected and autonomous vehicles. Our results were verified via WMG's user-in-the-loop simulations, in which state-of-the-art ray tracing simulations are combined with emulation of a physical signal. Use the slider to control the number of antennas in the network and see how the coverage changes.
